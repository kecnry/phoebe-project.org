Common wisdom

Exclusive RV fitting: to correctly determine mass ratio, it is wise to fit only the radial velocity data. There is a very important caveat you must be aware of while doing this: if you try to fit only the mass ratio while you keep inclination and semi-major axis fixed, you most probably won't obtain a proper solution!
